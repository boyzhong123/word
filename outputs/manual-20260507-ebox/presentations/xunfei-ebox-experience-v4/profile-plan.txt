Thesis:
EBOX Pro is best understood as a 2C family-side English learning terminal: it packages speech evaluation, English practice, parent control, and low-cost hardware into a daily home entrance. It does not currently replace a classroom listening-speaking training system, but it creates a credible upgrade path if iFLYTEK adds teacher-side and school-channel capabilities.
